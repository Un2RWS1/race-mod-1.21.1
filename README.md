# Minecraft, with Colors!
Bringing the world together (with multiplayer support) since 2026!

## Choose your color!
Choose your color and gain different buffs and debuffs to enhance your vanilla (or modded) experience.

# Disclaimer
For all intents and purposes, everything in this mod is satire. The stereotypes that are shown in this do not reflect our feelings towards each individual race. Some may be done due to balance changes, or general humor.
